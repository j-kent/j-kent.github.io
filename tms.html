Treasury Management System

Money Movement & Reconciliation

Knowing the exact status of payments and money movement is integral to a company like AvidXchange. Off-the-shelf software just couldn't cut-it for the demands that we had. Enter TMS...

The AvidXchange Treasury Management System was developed using the user-centered design process, and included many rounds of business requirements gathering, market / competitive analysis and feedback sessions involving our payment specialists and treasury team.

The result is robust tool that the treasury team at AvidXchange can have complete oversight into the exact state of every trust and account at any point in time.
