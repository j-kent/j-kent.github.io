Embudo

Audience Aggregation Engine


Embudo is a social search engine powered by a massive database of individuals that includes their social and driver data (name, address, work, email, phone & social handle). A user would be able to search and then segment and filter groups for marketing or research purposes.



Content Curation

Embudo also fatured a content curation module. This service allows a content professional to query trending content shared by social media thought leaders and push that content to various distribution channels including email campaigns, newsletters and content management systems.
